Renewable Energy Farmer

An idle farmer game themed on renewable sources of energy.

Start small by
having a couple of
renewable energy sources
to choose from.

Speed up the electricity
production by
buying more units
with the in-game currency.

The city is
fully powered by
a nuclear power plant
in the beginning.

Your goal is to
close the nuclear power plant
by powering the
city with renewable energy.

1 / 6
You will start the game with level 1 solar panel unit.
2 / 6
Here you can buy more units and exchange the stored energy for money.
3 / 6
The game cycles between day and nigh. Production speed is dynamically affected by time.
4 / 6
The nuclear power reactor will be closed when you'll unlock all the renewable energy sources.
5 / 6
Each day will start with a randomized weather. Your units react to the weather, by producing more / less energy.
6 / 6
This is how a fully renewable energy based city should look like! (End of the game)
❮ ❯

This game is meant to show that renewable sources of energy are a much better alternative to the harmful, non-renewable sources of energy.
